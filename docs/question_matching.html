There are four steps to clearing goods at the border. Place these in the correct order.

  • Getting permission to cross from the Immigration Desk
  • Clearing your products through Quality Control
  • Getting cleared at the Public Health Desk
  • Paying any fees required at the Customs Office
Verify Answers
  • First, you get your health and vaccination documents checked at the Public Health Desk;
  • Second, you get your passport/travel document validated by the Immigration Desk;
  • Third, you have your goods inspected by Quality Control;
  • Finally, you pay any fees required at the Customs Office.
